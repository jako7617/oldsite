02 Gathering data

Table of contents / Gathering data /

    Gathering data


    To start off, you will head to the “scan” view of the software in your newly created mission, and click the “New” button. Here, you will be asked to define the type of scan and shortly after, you will have the option to choose which plane, direction, polarisation and the amount of scans. These are details regarding the practical conducting of the test mission.


    You will now be presented with an overview of the scans you have created. The next step will be to create five cross-polarising scans. In order to proceed with the test, you must select the scan plans you wish to perform in a flight, then you export them.


    The software will then present you with an estimate of how much time it will take to perform the flight. If you are not satisfied with the estimate, you have the option to change the transfer speed. The software will then prompt you to choose where you want to export the files to.



    Guide

    Start:
    By double clicking on the name of the newly created mission on the bar on the left, you will be presented with the three views that you will interact with in order to perform the test.
